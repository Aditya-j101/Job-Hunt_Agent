Responsibilities and Impact:

Support Senior Data Scientists: Work closely with senior team members to assist in training, testing, and validating models. Help translate their mathematical logic into functional code.

Continuous Learning: Actively participate in technical discussions and model planning sessions to build your understanding of the AI lifecycles.

Workflow Automation: Identify manual data delivery tasks that slow the team down and practice your coding skills by converting them into reliable, automated Python pipelines.

Python Development: Write clean, efficient, and maintainable Python scripts to support daily analysis and model development.

Innovation & GenAI: Collaborate with the team to research and prototype using new libraries and frameworks to keep our tech stack on the cutting edge
